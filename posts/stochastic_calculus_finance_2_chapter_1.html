Introduction to Stochastic Calculus

Stochastic calculus is a branch of mathematics that operates on stochastic processes. It is used in various fields including finance. In this chapter, we will cover the basics of stochastic calculus.

Consider the stochastic differential equation:

$$ dX_t = \mu X_t dt + \sigma X_t dW_t $$

where \( X_t \) is the stochastic process, \( \mu \) is the drift coefficient, \( \sigma \) is the volatility coefficient, and \( W_t \) is the Wiener process or Brownian motion.
