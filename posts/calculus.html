Introduction to Calculus

Calculus is the mathematical study of continuous change. It has two major branches: differential calculus and integral calculus. Here is an example of a simple mathematical equation:

$$ f(x) = x^2 + 2x + 1 $$

The derivative of \( f(x) \) is:

$$ f'(x) = 2x + 2 $$
